Starfly

Non-human identity for the agentic fabric

WIMSE-profile JWTs, delegation policy, and revocation propagation — without touching the exchange hot path.

What it does

Starfly is the policy enforcement point for agent workloads. It issues and validates tokens, enforces delegation chains, ingests security event signals, and relays revocation state across federated fabric units.

Documentation and getting-started guides are shipping here and on starfly.dev/docs.
